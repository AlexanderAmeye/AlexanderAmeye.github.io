Introduction

Back in the late summer of 2019, I started looking into replicating a hand-drawn style within Unity. Specifically I wanted to successfully recreate the Ligne Claire style. The scene below was the first result in that attempt. It is a recreation of the panel from the right. This panel comes out The Black Island from The Adventures of Tintin created by Herg√©.



While I am fairly happy with how this scene turned out, the workflow of creating it was slow and convoluted while having several artistical restrictions. Specifically the method of drawing outlines provided me with little to no artistic control over where outlines were specifically drawn. The outline shader is good for drawing outlines on bigger geometry like the tunnel on the right, but when it comes to little details like the brick wall or the train, the shader was really hard to fine-tune. I will be revisiting this style in the future and optimizing the worklow to enable faster content creation.
