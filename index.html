Stylized Water For URP

For the past few months I have been working very hard on a stylized water shader for the Universal Render Pipeline. The result is a beautiful shader with features like depth-based colors, surface foam, intersection foam, caustics, waves, refraction, reflections and much more. More information here.

March 2020



Shaders Done Quick

I created some tutorials on the topic of shaders. The goal was to explain a certain shader in a short video of under 60 seconds. So far I have created a tutorial for a stylized water shader and a toon shader. The shaders themselves can be downloaded for free.

January 2020



Parallax Pokémon Cards

Parallax card shader created with shader graph in Unity. Except for the Pokémon 3D model everything is rendered onto 1 quad, with 1 shader and sampling only a single texture (for the text). More here.

December 2019



SDF Icons

SDF Pokémon icons created in shadergraph using signed distance functions. I created these to practice using SDFs in my shaders comfortably.

December 2019



The Wind Rises

Quick scene I created after watching 'The Wind Rises' by Studio Ghibli and being inspired by Japanese animation styles. Clouds painted in Photoshop, models made in Blender and rendered in Unity.
