Let's look at what I call 'sectioning'. We look at the entirety of the scene, divide it up in sections, give those sections to the outline shader which will draw lines between the sections. Adding visual enhancements

My current approach is to have 2 separate passes in the rendering process. First, I use a sectioning pass to generate a 'sectioning texture', then I do an outline pass that takes the sectioning texture as input, generates the outlines, and combines them with the camera color texture.
